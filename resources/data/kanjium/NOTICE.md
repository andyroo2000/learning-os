# Kanjium Pitch Accent Data

`accents.txt.gz` is a deterministic gzip copy of `accents.txt` from the
[Kanjium project](https://github.com/mifunetoshiro/kanjium)
and is distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0
International License. A copy of that license is in
`resources/licenses/KANJIUM-CC-BY-SA-4.0.txt`.

Required attribution:

> The pitch accent notation, verb particle data, phonetics, homonyms and other
> additions or modifications to EDICT, KANJIDIC or KRADFILE were provided by
> Uros O. through his free database.

Learning OS uses the file as an immutable lookup table and stores derived pitch
accent metadata on study cards.
